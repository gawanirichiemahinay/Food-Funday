About Us

Welcome to Food Funday – a place where great food, family, and fun come together!

Founded by Richie Pagapang in 2020, Food Funday is the culmination of a dream that began right here in the heart of the Philippines. As a graduate of the University of Cambridge, Richie’s journey into the world of food was inspired by his passion for creating memorable dining experiences for families. With a deep love for Filipino cuisine and a desire to bring people together over delicious meals, Food Funday was born.

From the very beginning, Richie’s vision for the restaurant was clear: to offer comforting, hearty dishes that are perfect for sharing with loved ones. At Food Funday, we take pride in serving a variety of family-friendly meals that highlight the rich flavors and diverse culinary traditions of the Philippines. Every dish is crafted with care, using only the finest ingredients, to ensure a dining experience that’s as heartwarming as it is flavorful.

At Food Funday, every meal is a celebration, and every visit feels like coming home. Whether you’re here for a casual lunch, a family gathering, or a special occasion, we invite you to enjoy the joy of good food in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Thank you for being part of our story – we look forward to making your next meal a funday!
